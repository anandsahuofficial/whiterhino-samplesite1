Radical Ion based NIR Probes




Radical Ion based NIR Probes


In 2009, we showed that NDI radical anions could be generated with organic amines. This redox reaction led to the differentiation of a large library of amines and applications of the NDI radical anions as multi-channel UV-VIS-NIR probes

M. R. Ajayakumar,et al
Chem. Commun. 2009, 3702 (Cover Article)


Subsequently, we also showed that cyanide anions could also reduce the NDI unit and form the radical anions. Thus, cyanide anion could be differentiated from a large number of anions including basic anions like Fluoride, Acetate, Phosphates, etc.

M. R. Ajayakumar,et al
Org. Lett. 2010, 12, 2646.
