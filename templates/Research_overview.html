Synthesis and Stabilization of Multi-Electron Acceptors and Multi-Electron Donors

Synthesis to enhance the electron donor ability of organic radicals has not been attempted due to instability, and reactivity challenges. We have now been able to synthesize and isolate, Naphthalenediimide-based (NDI·±) zwitterionic radicals, which bestow SOMO levels up to -4.04 eV.

Radical Ion based NIR Probes

In 2009, we showed that NDI radical anions could be generated with organic amines. This redox reaction led to the differentiation of a large library of amines and applications of the NDI radical anions as multi-channel UV-VIS-NIR probes
